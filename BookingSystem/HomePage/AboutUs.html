Welcome to Stay and Dine

Our Story

Stay and Dine was founded in 2015 with a vision to revolutionize the hospitality industry by offering seamless hotel and restaurant booking experiences. Starting as a small online platform in New York, we aimed to connect travelers with the finest accommodations and dining options. Over the years, we expanded globally, partnering with top hotels and restaurants to provide unparalleled service. Today, as of July 20, 2025, Stay and Dine proudly serves millions of customers worldwide, blending comfort and culinary excellence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create memorable experiences for our guests by offering a user-friendly platform that combines luxury stays with exquisite dining. We strive to ensure every booking is hassle-free and every meal is a delight.

Our Values


- Quality: Providing top-tier services and accommodations.

- Convenience: Simplifying the booking process.

- Excellence: Delivering exceptional customer satisfaction.
